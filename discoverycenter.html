Welcome to Discovery Science Center

At the discovery Science Center, we ignite curiosity and inspire the next generation of explorers. Through interactive exhibits, educational programs, and unforgettable experiences, we bring the wonders of science and space exploration to life.

Discover our features

Interactive exhibits

Dive into hands-on exhibits that make learning fun. From the mysteries of the cosmos to the intricacies of the human biology, our exhibits engage and educate visitors of all ages.

Space exploration gallery

Explore the history and future of space travel. Featuring artifacts from historic missions, including a special section dedicated to Buzz Aldrin and the Apollo 11 mission, our gallery celebrates human achievement and the spirit of exploration.

Plan your visit.

Open daily from 9 AM to 5 PM. Purchase your tickets online or at the door. Group discounts and annual memberships are available. Discover a world of wonder at the Discovery Science Center today!

Education programs

Enroll in our workshops, summer camps, and after-school programs designed to inspire young minds. Our programs cover a range of scientific topics, fostering a love of learning and discovery.

Special Events

Join us for lectures, star-gazing nights, and special events featuring renowned scientists and astronauts. Stay tuned to our events calendar to see what is coming up.

Planetarium shows

Experience the universe like never before in our state-of-the-art planetarium. Journey through space, explore distant galaxies, and learn about the latest discoveries in astronomy.

Membership

Become a member of the Discovery Science Center and enjoy exclusive benefits, including free admission, special event access, and more. Your support helps us continue our mission to educate and inspire.
